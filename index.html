A Guide to Gaming

Throughout our gaming journey from 2022 to 2024, we have curated a selection of three standout games from each year; each available on mobile, PC, and console. Discover the unique strengths of various platforms as we highlight the most captivating titles that have been released. Whether you’re playing on-the-go, immersively gaming at your desk, or experiencing gameplay on your console, there’s something for every type of gamer. Join us as we delve into the top games each year!

2022- The Year of Gaming

Game Title Description
Marvel Snap is a digital collectible card game created by Second Dinner and published by Nuverse for Android, iOS, and Windows. It includes a variety of characters from the Marvel Universe. The game officially launched on October 18, 2022, following a beta testing phase. ℹ
Elden Ring is an open world action role-playing game released in 2022, developed by FromSoftware and directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki. It was launched on February 25 for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S. The game revolves around the quest to restore the Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord, facing various factions, mythical creatures, and powerful bosses along the way. ℹ
God of War Ragnarök is a 2022 action-adventure game developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It was released worldwide on November 9, 2022, for both the PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5. The game continues the story of Kratos and his son Atreus as they navigate the challenges and consequences of Norse mythology. ℹ

2023- Unmissable Titles of the Year

Game Title Description
Hitman: Blood Money — Reprisal is a mobile game developed and released by Feral Interactive and will be 2023 on iOS (App Store) Android (Google Play) November 30, is the mobile port version of Blood Money, the fourth game in the series. The release date of the Nintendo Switch port is January 25, 2024. ℹ
Baldur's Gate 3 is a 2023 role-playing video game developed and published by Larian Studios. It is the third main installment to the Baldur's Gate series, based on the tabletop fantasy role-playing system of Dungeons & Dragons.Fully released for Windows in August 2023, with versions for PlayStation 5, macOS, and Xbox Series X/S releasing later that year. ℹ
The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom[b] is a 2023 action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch. The player controls Link as he searches for Princess Zelda and fights to prevent Ganondorf from destroying Hyrule. ℹ

2024- Innovations that Redefined Gaming

Game Title Description
Call of Duty: Warzone Mobile is a 2024 free-to-play mobile first-person shooter game co-developed by Digital Legends Entertainment, Beenox, Activision Shanghai Studio, and Solid State Studios, and published by Activision. It launched on March 21, 2024, for iOS and Android. ℹ
Satisfactory is a 2024 factory simulation game, available on PC. It was created by Swedish video game developer Coffee Stain Studios. The game is a 3D first-person open world exploration and factory building game. ℹ
Astro Bot is a platform game developed by Team Asobi and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment for the PlayStation 5, released in 2024. It serves as a sequel to Astro's Playroom (2020) and is the third installment in the Astro Bot series. ℹ
